Details by product

EyesOfNetwork

The Open Source solution pragmatically bringing together the ITIL processes and the actors of the information system.

Supervise all elements of the infrastructure

EyesOfNetwork is a global supervision solution for the hardware status of equipment, operating systems, standard applications, business applications and performance.

Map your information system

Mapping makes it possible to popularize supervision information. It is thus possible to offer the views corresponding to everyone's expectations (geomap, technical and business views).

Alert in case of anomaly

Raise alerts to a targeted population in the event of a defect in elements of your information system. Thanks to advanced notifications, graphically define your notification rules so that you can intervene as efficiently as possible.

Manage your operating department

The EON production module ensures the main processes necessary to keep your infrastructure in operational condition (event, change, problem, asset, administrative and technical data management).

Measure the quality of service

The reports generated by Eyes Of Network allow you to assess the availability of your applications, determine the quality of your services with the technical SLA and save you time thanks to its document rendering engine.

Easily deploy

Several installation modes are available: Ansible, ISO Image or even by group of RPM packages. So whatever your target environment, you will find a deployment method adapted to your needs.

EyesOfApplication

Measure the quality of service provided to end users with EyesOfApplication

Monitor your applications

Eyes Of Application allows you to best measure the user experience in terms of service availability, application performance and data integrity.

Model your user scenarios

Creation of application robots based on scripts simulating the current actions of users while remaining faithful to the operating context. Applications hosted on heterogeneous platforms from a user workstation: connection test, database access, ...

Measure availability

Thanks to the sequenced execution, each user action is measured to obtain precise performance indicators. Find all performance and availability information from Grafana.

Identify unavailability

Eyes Of Application allows rapid identification of the criticality of incidents, the sites or services concerned, the total number of users affected, the duration of degradations or unavailability. Identification of the problems and proposal of an axis of resolution.

Operation

Define a probe station representative of your users' environment (Windows / Linux) and install EyesOfApplication on it. Design scenarios to measure the availability and performance of your users' actions. No matter the programming language or the access mode to your applications (Web / Thick client), EyesOfApplication will execute either the scenario by image recognition for thick clients, or by browsing the code in Web mode thanks to Selenium.

Le rôle d'EyesOfApplication au sein de la gamme Eyes

The application supervision carried out by EyesOfApplication makes it possible to enrich the modeling of "Business Processes", thus making notifications and availability reports more relevant.

EyesOfReport

The solution to manage the quality of your services

Enhance your supervision tools

EyesOfReport interfaces with your supervision tools, in order to integrate availability information for all the equipment in your infrastructure.

Model your applications

The proper functioning of your applications is directly linked to the health of the equipment that hosts them and connects them to end users. Intuitively model the relationship between your applications and your infrastructure in the Business Process Management module.

Formalize your catalog of services

Define your service contracts (client, sla, time covers, ...) in our contract management module and link them to your applications to build your service catalog.

Analyze, improve, communicate

A certain number of exportable reports in the format of your choice (pdf, html, Word, powerpoint ...) will allow you to visualize the availability of your applications, analyze the causes of degraded services and prepare your steering committees.

Le rôle d'EyesOfReport au sein de la gamme Eyes

The ideal tool for modeling and representing your service catalog. EyesOfReport relies in particular on EyesOfNetwork for the recovery of service reports necessary for building reports.

EyesOfLog

A solution responding to a multitude of use cases (logs, SIEM, APM, IA, ...)

Centralize

Collect the logs from your information system. This information retrieved by agents or by standard protocol is full of essential information, both on security, on operation and on the carrying capacity of your environments.

Analyze behaviors

Thanks to the functionality of Machine Learning , detect anomalies in the behavior of your users and intervene manually or automatically before it is too late.

Analyze network flows

Analyze packets in transit on your environments. Understand how your applications work and be proactive in case of compromise.

Capacity Planing

By gathering information from logs and performance metrics, build up an information database allowing you to apply Machine Learning to forecast infrastructure developments.

Application performance

With the APM functionality, analyze the transactions within your applications and identify delays and errors that could impact your users. Pair this module with machine learning, and you will be alerted before your users are impacted.

Investigate

Browse your data from all your infrastructure components (Firewall, Routers, Switches, Servers, Applications), select a time range, and drill in the event layers.

Search engine

Speed up your applications by using EyesOfLog as a "Backend" for your applications. Vacuum the data from your historical databases (DB2, Oracle, ...) and redirect your applications to EyesOfLog in order to benefit from high application performance.

Predictive maintenance

Analyze your data from your equipment in order to predict failures on your equipment. So be proactive in managing your infrastructure and minimize user impacts while anticipating your budget.

Le rôle d'EyesOfLog au sein de la gamme Eyes

EyesOfLog enriches the traditional monitoring model through dynamic monitoring and capacity planning. EyesOfLog also allows the product range to be closer to the application layer.
